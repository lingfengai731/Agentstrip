Travel, reimagined — when six minds plan as one.

WanderMind Studio was born from a simple frustration: planning a great trip still takes hours of switching between blog posts, booking sites, weather apps, and currency converters — only to end up with a generic itinerary that doesn't really fit you.

We built six AI agents — a trip planner, an accommodation advisor, a food explorer, an activity curator, a budget manager, and a live web search — and taught them to collaborate. The result is a single conversation where every detail of your journey is considered, refined, and personalised. From Bali to Kyoto to Paris to Santorini, or any city you type in, your trip is built in minutes, not weekends.

Six AI agents. Five languages. Every city on Earth.

We are a small team focused on one big idea: travel planning should feel like talking to a knowledgeable friend, not filling out forms. Our agents are always on duty, drawing from live weather data, real-time hotel and flight prices, and curated local intelligence from every corner of the globe.

Meet the Six AI Agents

Each agent has a single area of expertise — and together, they make sure no detail of your journey goes overlooked.

Trip Planner

Itinerary architect

Designs day-by-day routes, optimises travel time between sights, and adapts pacing to your energy and interests.

Hotel Advisor

Where to stay

Compares real-time hotel prices, suggests stays by neighbourhood character, and balances budget against location.

Food Explorer

Local cuisine guide

Surfaces hidden local eateries, signature dishes, and dietary-aware options — beyond the tourist menus.

Activity Curator

Experiences specialist

Recommends experiences matched to your style — from sunrise hikes to wine tastings to traditional ceremonies.

Budget Manager

Cost optimisation

Estimates daily spend, tracks live exchange rates, and finds where to save without compromising the experience.

Live Web Search

Real-time intelligence

Pulls current visa policies, weather forecasts, event schedules, and local news to keep every answer up to date.

Trusted local partners

Our AI is always on, but sometimes a human friend on the ground makes all the difference. We work with vetted local guides in our key destinations.
